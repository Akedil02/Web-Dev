Mapo tofu

  • Time: 5
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
tofu 500 grams
beef 100 grams
sugar 10 grams

Step 1:

  1. Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  2. 1/4 teaspoon of ground white pepper with minced beef.
  3. Cut tofu into square cubes (around 2cm).
  4. Bring a large amount of water to a boil and then add a pinch of salt. Slide the tofu in and cook for 1 minute.

Source: Taste of home

Butter chicken

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 15
Ingredients Quantity
chicken 500 grams
butter 50 grams
chili 50 grams

Step 1:

  1. Make Butter Chicken Marinade and marinate chicken for 3+ hours (overnight is best)
  2. Cook chicken
  3. Make sauce in the same skillet
